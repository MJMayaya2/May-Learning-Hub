Hi, I'm MJ Mayaya.

Welcome to May Learning Hub. I’m an educator dedicated to transforming how we teach and learn. I have been in the classroom since 2018, having graduated from the Edgewood campus, and my primary focus is bringing Social Sciences, Geography, and Life Sciences to life for high school learners.

May Learning Hub was built from a simple idea: learning should not end when the bell rings. Learners should be able to revisit content, activities, and revision tools wherever they are.

Education in Your Pocket

We live in a digital world, and I believe the best way to teach is to meet students where they already are—on their phones, tablets, and computers. I have a deep passion for technology, and I built this platform to blend that passion with education.

Think of this site as “notes in your pocket.” By making learning mobile, beautifully designed, and interactive, students can access their materials, activities, and custom-built learning games anywhere and at any time.

Synchronizing the Classroom

This platform is not only for learners; it is also designed to make teaching easier for fellow educators. The site features tailor-made, CAPS-aligned resources that can support classroom teaching in a practical and structured way.

To make complex topics easier to understand, the resources are supported by clear visuals, diagrams, guided content, and interactive learning tools. When teachers present from this hub and learners follow along on their own devices, it creates a more synchronized educational journey.

Why This Matters

Good teaching is not only about explaining content. It is also about presentation, clarity, accessibility, and engagement. My goal is to build resources that help teachers teach better and help learners understand more confidently.

I want this platform to reduce the distance between the classroom and the learner’s everyday world by making quality educational material easier to reach, easier to use, and easier to enjoy.
